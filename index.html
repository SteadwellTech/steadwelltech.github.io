Steadwell · Washington, DC

Learn. Make. Become.

Steadwell builds tools for a life well-lived.

The best life is a passionate one.

We live in a moment that makes passion harder to act on. The pace is relentless. The tools that were supposed to free us have made whole fields of human expertise feel obsolete overnight. And somewhere in the noise, the question of what you actually want — what you'd pursue if the path were clear — gets harder to hear.

Steadwell exists to help you hear it. And then to help you act on it.

Our Approach

Technology that makes you more yourself.

Every Steadwell product is grounded in how people actually learn and grow — not in what's fastest or most automated, but in what produces real, lasting change in real human beings.

We're students of learning science, positive psychology, and the kind of deep expertise that only comes from sustained, deliberate practice. We build tools that meet you where you are, take your goals seriously, and stay out of the way when you don't need them.

This is the opposite of outsourcing yourself to a machine. It's using technology to become more fully who you are.

Our First Product

Language is one of the most human things there is.

It connects us to places, to people, to versions of ourselves we haven't become yet. The best way to learn a language isn't in a classroom — it's in the world, in the places where that language lives and breathes.

Now in Development · Washington, DC

Langly

Langly helps language learners and newcomers find real-world immersion opportunities close to home — the coffee shop where Spanish flows, the market where Mandarin fills the air, the neighborhood where English feels welcoming.

It's a mobile app for discovering the living language of your city, built on the belief that the best classroom is the world around you.

Launching first in Washington, DC. No spam, ever.

Who We Are

Built by someone who needed it.

I started coding in high school, studied linguistics in college, and spent years afterward translating and analyzing language professionally — work I loved until I didn't. What I'd cherished about language was the learning, the meaning-making, the art of expression. When that work became a race against machines, the art disappeared. I still loved software, so I refreshed my skills and made the switch. It's been genuinely good — I enjoy solving problems, and I'm decent at it.

But AI is coming for that too. And honestly, when I look at what I actually care about — what I'd pursue if the path were clear — it's learning, growth, and the craft of becoming more fully yourself. That's what led me here.

Steadwell is my attempt to build something at the intersection of all of it: tools grounded in how people actually learn, designed for people who want to grow rather than be replaced, made with the care that meaningful work deserves. I build these apps because I need them. That's where every Steadwell product starts.

— Joseph Marulli, Founder · Washington, DC
